Lata Mangeshkar

Nightingale of India

Here's a timeline of Lata Mangeshkar 's life:

  • 1929 - Born in Indore, Madhya Pradesh, India.
  • 1941 - She started her music career at the age of 12.
  • 1949 - Song from the movie "Mahal" was her first major hit.
  • 1963 - Her "Aye mere watan ke logon" brought tears to every indian during the Sino-Indian war.
  • 1989 - She received, Dadasaheb Phalke Award.
  • 1997 - She received state government's highest civilian award - Maharashtra Bhushan Award.
  • 2001 - Mangeshkar received India's highest civilian award - The Bharat Ratna Award for her contribution to music.
  • 2015 - Her last song for a movie was "Jeena hai kya"
  • 2022 - Dies at the age of 92, in Mumbai, India.
